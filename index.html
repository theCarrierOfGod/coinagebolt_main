Why CoinageBolt

Speed, security, scalability

CoinageBolt’s aBFT consensus protocol delivers unparalleled speed, security, and reliability.
Enjoy almost instant transactions and extremely low fees.

Almost instant transfers

Transactions on CoinageBolt are finalized in a second and cost a fraction of a cent.

Simply secure

CoinageBolt’s validator nodes form a global, trustless, and leaderless Proof-of-Stake network.

Highly scalable

CoinageBolt can process thousands of transactions per seconds and scale to thousands of nodes.

Ethereum compatible

CoinageBolt is EVM compatible. Deploy and run your Ethereum dApps on Fantom.
